Wildlife of Australia

Australia is known for its iconic native animals.

Due to the countries wide range of environments from the desert, to all the way to the bush. Many animals have been able to call Australia its home. Just like the Platypus who are found in Eastern Australia. From the tropics of North Queensland to the cold of Tasmania. The Platypus is known for its duck like bill and being one of very few mammals who can lay eggs.

Fun Fact

The Kangaroo is a part of the Marsupial (maa·soo·pee·uhl) family called Macropods. Animals like wallabies and tree-kangaroos are also included as a part of this family!
